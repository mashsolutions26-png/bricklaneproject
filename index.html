About Us

A corner of the lane
we made our own.

Brick Lane is the ultimate bakery-café destination in Melbourne's CBD. With a beautiful brick facade, lush greenery and neon signage, our contemporary menu is designed to change with the seasons.

Using fresh and sustainable local produce, everything on the Brick Lane menu is full of flavour and prepared directly on site, so you enjoy it when it is at its best.

We roast in small batches, bake daily, and keep the menu tight so everything on it is done properly. Come early, the good tables go fast.
